================================================================================
SKILL TREES — COMBAT MECHANICS EDITION (turn-based, hex-based, balanced)
================================================================================
This version aligns descriptions with turn-based combat:
  - Time: turns only (no seconds). Cooldowns in turns.
  - Distance: hexes only (no meters).
  - Action economy: "AP" = action points (how many actions per turn); "initiative"
    = turn order. Buffs that "give more actions" grant AP at turn start.
  - More impactful numbers: tier 1 modest, tier 5 dramatic. Buffs 40–100% where
    appropriate; durations 3–5 turns unless the effect is extreme.
  - Lower tier = weaker; higher tier = stronger. Skills moved or scaled as needed.
================================================================================
